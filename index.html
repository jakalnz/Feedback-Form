COMPETENCY RATING KEY: 1: ABSENT (No experience/unable to perform) | 2: EMERGING (Has knowledge, needs direct instruction) | 3: PRESENT (Needs monitoring barring routine cases) | 4: DEVELOPED (Monitoring only for difficult cases) | 5: CONSISTENT (Well developed, supervisor as consultant)
Objective / Criteria 1 2 3 4 5
Ia - Ethical and Professional Behaviour Punctual, culturally sensitive, respects confidentiality, ethical limits.
Ib - Interpersonal Behaviour Communication skills, builds trust, responds to cues, manages unexpected events.
Ic - Refining Behaviour Reflective practice, receptive to feedback, generates ideas for improvement.
II - Effective Session Preparation Reads records, identifies issues, plans interview/tests appropriately.
III - Effective Session Management Client interaction, Assessment Selection, Time Management.
IV - Effective Session Reasoning & Integration Integrates info, recognises inconsistencies, forms impression, appropriate referrals.
Va - Session Interview Hx Taking, Needs assessment, client centered approach.
Vb - Testing PTA, Immittance, Speech Mapping. Technically competent & accurate.
Vc - Presentation of Findings Presents findings accurately, clear recommendations/orientation.
Vd - Clinical Writing Skills Technical accuracy, logical format, complete file info, timely submission.
